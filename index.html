About Team
Welcome to Forest Invaders !
Forest Invaders is a game where you, a fire extinguisher, must put out the incoming fires and avoid spraying animals
Player

The player can be various things, from a fire extinguisher to a slightly larger fire extinguisher, with 4 characters total.

They can be moved with A and D, or the left and right arrow keys. The spacebar is used shoot to extinguish enemies.

Fires

The fires are the main challenge of the game, which you must clear out before they get past you. Otherwise, you lose and the game ends. They serve as enemies, while others are peaceful.

Original Art

One of the top feature of this game is our original art ! Our sprite designer, Justin has created a beuftiful, indie-like pixel art design !
